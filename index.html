STRATO

Custom hobby rocket flight computer built on a LilyGO T-Beam (ESP32 + GPS + LoRa + OLED). Full flight state machine with BMP280 barometric sensing, LoRa telemetry to a ground receiver, OLED HUD, and an ESP32-CAM for onboard video. Resolved I2C bus conflicts, SH1106 OLED driver issues, and TX/RX pin conflicts along the way.

Background

About Me

I'm a high school student in North Brunswick, New Jersey building an engineering portfolio aimed at aerospace and mechanical engineering. I've been part of Hack Club's Blueprint and Stasis programs, where I've shipped hardware projects ranging from rocket flight computers to autonomous agricultural UAVs.

My focus is on physical building over software — I care about the mechanical design of a motor mount, the thermal behavior of a flight computer enclosure, and the wiring decisions that separate a clean build from a rats' nest. I use tools like Python and INAV as means to an end, not the end itself.

My long-term goal is to study aerospace or mechanical engineering and work on real propulsion, autonomy, or structural systems — the kind of work that actually flies.
